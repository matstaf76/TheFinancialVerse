Meet the Ancillary Aliens!

Ancillary Aliens are slimy space creatures that burst out of bags and planes. They multiply like a hive and add surprise extras, often sent by Jack the Ruiner!

What They Represent in Real Life

In real life, Ancillary Aliens are like airline extra fees. You buy a plane ticket for $100, but then they charge $25 more for your seat or bag. It's like paying for a ride but extra for sitting down! Check all costs when flying to zap these aliens away.
