The Ancillary Aliens

Sneaky Minions of Hidden Fees!

I don't know where they came from but they didn't used to be such a problem and they get worse all the time!

The Ancillary Aliens are slimy creatures that burst out of travel and recreation plans! They multiply like a hive and spread like a plague adding surprise extras, sent by a Jack the Ruiner!

In real life, Ancillary Aliens are like "convenience fees" on concert tickets or airline extra fees. on your travel plans. You buy a plane ticket for $100, but then they charge $25 more for your seat or bag. It's like paying for a ride but extra for sitting down! Check all costs when flying to zap these aliens away.

The only way we can defeat them is TOGETHER! Check business reviews about who charges for what. Do research and specifically ASK about all fees or extra charges. It is important to ask about what is COMMON BUT NOT INCLUDED in your purchases and plans. DO NOT give your business to companies caught that charging hidden fees. If it happens to you be sure to leave a review somewhere telling others! Knowledge is your shield.
