Meet The Subscriptor!

The Subscriptor seduces with promises of something useful at a small recurring fee. The villain turns free trials into never-ending payments!

What He Represents in Real Life

In real life, The Subscriptor is like subscription fees that keep going. You try a game app for free, but forget to cancel, and it takes $10 every month! It's like a toy that keeps asking for money. Set reminders to cancel trials and beat him.
