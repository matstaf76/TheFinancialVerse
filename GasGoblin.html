Meet Gas Goblin!

Gas Goblin is a cackling flyer on a glider who throws exploding bombs of unpredictable costs. He haunts digital money worlds like crypto!

What He Represents in Real Life

In real life, Gas Goblin is like gas or transaction fees in online money. When you trade digital coins, extra fees pop up like $10 surprises. It's like paying to play a video game but extra for each move! Learn about low-fee options to goblin-proof your trades.
