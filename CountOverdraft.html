Meet Count Overdraft!

Count Overdraft is a sneaky vampire-like villain in a tattered robe. He hides in the shadows of your bank account and bites when you spend just a tiny bit more money than you have!

What He Represents in Real Life

In real life, Count Overdraft is like overdraft fees. Imagine you have $5 in your piggy bank and try to buy a $6 candy. The bank says okay but then charges you extra money, like $30 more! It's a sneaky fee that makes small mistakes cost a lot. Always check your money before spending to beat him!
