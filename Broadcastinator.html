Meet The Broadcastinator!

The Broadcastinator is a masked giant who pumps up bills with add-ons. He crushes fun TV watching with additional fees like sneaky equipment charges!

What He Represents in Real Life

In real life, The Broadcastinator is like broadcast or sports TV fees. Your family pays $50 for cable, but adds $20 more for sports channels or a box. It's like paying for cartoons but extra for the remote! Ask for simple plans to terminate him.
