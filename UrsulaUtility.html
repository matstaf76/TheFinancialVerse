Ursula Utility

Queen of Extra Service Fees and Irregular Billing!

Ursula Utility controls the flow of water and electricity. She is always finding ways to sneak in volatility and extra charges that hit your bills unexpectedly.

You can't live witout her in the modern world AND SHE KNOWS IT!

Real-world equivalents include service fees, late charges, delivery fees, supply charges or variable rate hikes. She ensures your bills are never quite what you expect.

You cannot defeat her! She is modern services! To live with her YOU MUST monitor bills, dispute unnecessary charges and price spikes. ALWAYS practice mindful consumption. Awareness is your power against Ursula but we are often trapped in her tentacles!
