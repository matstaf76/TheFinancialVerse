Meet Ursula Utility!

Ursula Utility is a tentacled witch under the sea who tricks with required modern services. She snares people in high, often erratic and confusing utility bills!

What She Represents in Real Life

In real life, Ursula Utility is like utility or water fees. Your home pays $30 for water or power, but adds extras for "supply" or "delivery" you don't understand. It's like paying for a bath water but also for the faucet and pipes again! Check bills and save water to dry up her tricks.
