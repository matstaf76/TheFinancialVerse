Meet ATM the Barbarian!

ATM the Barbarian is a wild warlord on a horse who raids your money with high penalties. He conquers when you use the wrong cash machine!

What He Represents in Real Life

In real life, ATM the Barbarian is like ATM or transfer fees. You take $20 from a machine not your bank's, and it charges $3 extra! It's like a bully taking part of your allowance. Use your own bank's ATM to keep him away.
